# scalar-QNMs-higher-derivative-gravity
Lowest order corrections to scalar quasinormal modes in higher derivative gravity theories, more specifically scalar Gauss Bonnet, dynamical Chern Simons and Cubic gravity.

This repository accompanies the following papers:

-  S.J.B. Husken, T. van der Steen, S. Maenaut, K.K.H. Lam, M.D. Jockwer, A.K.W. Chung, T. Hertog, T.G.F. Li, and N. Yunes,
  Quadratic gravity corrections to scalar QNMs of rapidly rotating black holes (2026), [arXiv:2604.02214 [gr-qc]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02214) .

-  T. van der Steen, S. Maenaut, S.J.B. Husken, P.G.S. Fernandes, M.D. Jockwer, V. Cardoso, T. Hertog, and T G.F. Li,
  Ringing of rapidly rotating black holes in effective field theory (2026), [arXiv:2604.11755 [gr-qc]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.11755) .

Discaimer: More results for scalar Gauss Bonnet and dynamical Chern Simons will be added to this repository in the near future.
